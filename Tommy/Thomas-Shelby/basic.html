Who is Thomas Shelby?

What can I say, he is the mastermind of the great Peaky Blinders.

Why is he so special and different from other characters in any show till now?

His way of dealing with problems and his persona is what defines him. He has seen a lot of bloodshed and trauma during the first world war. The war has changed him a lot. Almost everything has become a joke to him. Superiority is a joke, Authority is a joke. He is his own revolution.
